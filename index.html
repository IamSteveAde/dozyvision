About Mmobuosi Vision Ltd

Mmobuosi Vision Ltd is a creative and intellectual property development company dedicated to producing high-impact media that celebrates African innovation, leadership, and global influence. We craft stories that inspire and build cultural assets with lasting value.

Our Mission

To chronicle, celebrate, and amplify Africa’s stories through film, documentary, and literary works — empowering a new generation of innovators, entrepreneurs, and cultural leaders across the continent and the diaspora.

2023 — Founded

Established as a special-purpose vehicle for a proposed sports acquisition.

Pivot to IP & Media

Evolved into an intellectual property and media production company focused on African narratives.

Flagship Initiative

Developing the Dozy Mmobuosi Memoir Project across film, documentary, and print.

Core Values

  • Authenticity: Truthful storytelling rooted in lived experience.
  • Excellence: Global-quality standards guide every production.
  • Impact: Stories that create cultural and social value.
  • Collaboration: Partnerships with creators and cultural leaders.
What We Do

Stories that shape the future

Film & Documentary Production

We produce world-class motion pictures and documentaries that highlight African innovation, leadership, and global influence — capturing real stories that inspire progress and unity.

Intellectual Property Development

From books to screenplays, we cultivate creative concepts and own the intellectual rights to groundbreaking African stories — ensuring authenticity and cultural excellence in global storytelling.

Cultural Narratives & Media Strategy

We develop powerful narratives and visual campaigns that amplify Africa’s voice, shaping global conversations about leadership, innovation, and the African experience.

Brand Storytelling & Partnerships

Through strategic collaborations and brand-driven storytelling, we bring African excellence to the global stage — connecting purpose, media, and innovation for lasting impact.
